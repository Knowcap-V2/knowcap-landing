The Problem

Service firms leak 20–40% of project hours due to undocumented work and unverifiable decisions

Gartner predicts >70% of ERP initiatives fail to meet original business goals due to poor alignment and scope drift.

1
Process drift

Juniors skip or sequence steps incorrectly.

2
Scope creep

Clients "remember" extra work without proof.

3
Manual documentation

2–3 hours/meeting rewriting notes.

4
Knowledge loss

Seniors repeatedly explain the same workflows.

Impact: Payroll is 70–90% of cost; losing 10% of billable hours can erase 100% of project profit.

The Solution

Knowcap recovers 30% of total delivery time by turning real work into verifiable, timestamped documentation

  • ✓ Captures context: call participation, real-time screen observation, document uploads
  • ✓ Understands execution: detects fields, clicks, steps, configurations
  • ✓ Compares against expectations: matches actions to SOPs, requirements, and agreements
  • ✓ Flags drifts: Project is At Risk from the original scope
  • ✓ Generates verifiable artifacts: contracts, quotations, SOPs, gap analyses

Result: Improves accuracy, removes rework loops, and reveals project risks early.
